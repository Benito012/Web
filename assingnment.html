History For Tomorrow

The History for Tomorrow is a website designed to educate the upcoming generation about the national disaster we faced as South Africa from 2022 till today. It is also a reflection and a memorial for our brothers and sisters we lost during the gender-based violence crisis. This website holds memories we can all return to and ensure they are never forgotten. We created a safe space that does not choose gender, age or race.
